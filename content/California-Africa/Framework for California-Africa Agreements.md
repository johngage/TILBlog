title: Framework for California-Africa Agreements
---
Meetings in California during Climate Week between climate envoys from Nigeria and Kenya, California Secretary of Transportation Tocs Omishakin, Governor Gavin Newsom, and the heads of California major executive departments explored future agreements.

A plan evolved to create a California-Nigeria, California-Kenya, and California-Ghana set of memoranda of understanding, setting out plans for cooperation in investment, trade, education and research.

Institutions involved: 
- San Francisco Climate Week
- California-Africa Bay Area Council
- Bakar Institute Climate and AI Seminar
